Description

The Electromobility ontology represents and organizes knowledge about the charging infrastructure for electric vehicles. This ontology defines concepts and relations that are relevant to the charging infrastructure - such as pool, station, charging point and connector- but do not cover electric vehicle characteritics. Roles of stakeholders of the charging infrastructure are further described in the ROLE ontology. Detailed characteristics of charging equipments and sites are given in the PROPERTY module.
