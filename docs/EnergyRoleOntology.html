Energy Role Ontology
Description

Purpose: The Energy Role ontology is dedicated to business parties, their intended behaviour, and relationships with other elements involved in energy market business processes. Scope: The Energy Role ontology defines three generic concepts: business parties (participant), business roles (role), and their relationships (association). Roles are further refined in classifications specific to electromobility, flexibility, and local energy communities domains.
